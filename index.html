About Blue Cardamon

Welcome. I'm Elizabeth Brennan an artist and designer based in Dublin, Ireland. I mostly work in Film, but in my spare time I enjoy making beautiful custom cards.

I'm a mother and want the Planet to be a better place for everones kids. I think the best way to achieve this is re-cycling, up-cycling, and buying local.
